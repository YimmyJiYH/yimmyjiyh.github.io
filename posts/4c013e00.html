:blue_book: 摘要：The paper finds many interesting conclusions. And the most impressive result is that the impact of supervisors on attrition is much more significant than the impact of salary: increasing salary by 20% would decrease the voluntary attrition by 5%, whereas changing agents’ supervisors to the best supervisor would decrease the voluntary attrition by 10%. The greatest contribution of the paper is the comparison between raising salary and changing supervisors.
